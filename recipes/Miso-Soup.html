Miso Soup

Description

Making miso soup at home is one of the simplest and most satisfying ways to treat yourself during ...

it will take around 20 mins to get it ready

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (2-inch) piece kombu (dried black kelp)
  • 1/2 cup loosely packed dried bonito flakes (katsuobushi), optional
  • 4 ounces silken or firm tofu, drained
  • 1 to 2 medium scallions

Steps

  1. Make the dashi
  2. Prepare the tofu and scallions
  3. Bring the broth to a rapid simmer
  4. Mix the miso with 1/2 cup hot broth
  5. Pour the miso into the broth
  6. Add the tofu
  7. Add the scallions
  8. Serve in individual bowls
