Mashroom Pasta

Description

this eazy to do Pasta with mashrooms is a very quick way to make you famaily happy

it will take you 10 minuts to get ready and 20 min until the food is ready on your table

Ingredients

  • Pasta (what ever type like like)

  • parmezan

Steps

  1. Put the PAsta in hot water until cooked
  2. take the water out
  3. add the mashrooms

  5. cut the Cherry tomatoes and add them
  6. coock for 15 min and its ready
