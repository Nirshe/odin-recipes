Apple Cake

Description

This is a NICE German apple cake, it not easy but the taste is one of the best!

it will take 1.5h to get it ready

Ingredients

  • 10 Green Apples
  • 1GK Flower
  • 250ml Milk
  • 1 liter Water
  • 500g Soger

Steps

  1. Make the doaw for the cake
  2. Put the apple and the soger
  3. Get the oven to 180 C temp
  4. Put soger on the cake
  5. Put in the oven for 1 hour
